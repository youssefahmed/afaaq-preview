About Us

Afaq Al-Tamayoz Industry

A leading manufacturer and supplier of polystyrene solutions in Saudi Arabia and beyond.

+20 years of experience

Industrial expertise and certified quality

Afaq Al-Tamayoz combines modern production technology with long technical experience to deliver reliable polystyrene products and services for projects inside and outside the Kingdom.

  • Advanced technologies and modern production machinery.
  • Administrative and technical expertise extending for more than twenty years.
  • Periodic samples are sent to Saudi standards authorities to ensure quality.

These factors helped the company stand out in production quality, efficiency, and competitive pricing. With growing demand for polystyrene products, the new factory was established in 2018 in Al Kharj Industrial City and equipped with advanced global production lines.
